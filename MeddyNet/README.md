# 🏥 MeddyNet — National Home Diagnostics Network
> **"Making Healthcare Testing as Easy as Ordering Food"**

MeddyNet is India's premier healthcare technology platform, a comprehensive digital ecosystem connecting patients, diagnostic laboratories, pharmacies, hospitals, and technicians. We prioritize convenience, transparency, and quality to build India's largest decentralized healthcare infrastructure.

---

## 📋 1. Executive Summary
The National Home Diagnostics Network (NHDN) creates a nationwide network of existing labs and healthcare providers, standardizing quality and enabling seamless access.
- **Unified Booking:** Tests, medicines, and doctors in one app.
- **Home-First Model:** Bringing diagnostics and pharmacy to the doorstep.
- **Data Centrality:** Lifetime secure digital health records.
- **Goal:** Build India’s largest decentralized diagnostics and healthcare service network.

---

## 🌋 2. Problem Statement
Healthcare diagnostics in India is currently fragmented and inconvenient:
1. **Long Queues:** Hours of waiting for basic tests.
2. **Travel Barriers:** Especially difficult for the elderly and sick.
3. **Rural Gaps:** Lack of reliable infrastructure in small towns.
4. **Scattered Records:** No central access to historical health data.
5. **Transparency Lack:** Pricing and quality vary significantly without easy comparison.

---

## 📈 3. Market Opportunity
India's diagnostics industry is valued at **₹1 Lakh Crore+**, growing at **10–14% annually**. While competitors focus on owning labs, MeddyNet focuses on **network aggregation**, a scalable model with massive underserved potential in Tier 2/3 cities.

## 🏗️ 5. Platform Architecture
MeddyNet is built as a three-tier modular system:

### 📱 User Application
- **Discovery:** Test search, Lab comparison, Map-view.
- **Management:** Booking engine, Health records, Medicine ordering.
- **Support:** Doctor consults, Emergency alerts.

### 🧪 Lab Partner Dashboard
- **Inventory:** Manage test menus, listings, and reactive pricing.
- **Logistics:** Track incoming samples and verify technician deliveries.
- **Reporting:** Secure report generation and digital upload.

### 🛵 Technician Application
- **Operations:** Appointment schedules and route navigation.
- **Verification:** Collection logs and payment tracking.

## 💰 6. Business Model & Strategy

### 6.1 Revenue Pipeline
1. **Transactional Commission:** 20–30% per test booking.
2. **MeddyPlus Subscriptions:** Monthly monitoring packages.
3. **Corporate Contracts:** Enterprise health monitoring fees.

## ⚙️ 8. Technical Blueprint

### 8.1 System Logic
- **Search & Map:** Filters labs within a specific radius, checks availability, and sorts by price/distance.
- **Onboarding Flow:** Lab Submit Details → Verification → Live on Network.

### 8.2 Database Entities
- **Users:** Profiles, contacts, health vault.
- **Labs & Tests:** Location, pricing, availability, certifications.
- **Bookings:** Status (Pending → Collected → Delivered → Result Ready).

### 8.3 Blueprint Screens
- **User (~10):** Home, Search, Map, Comparison, Booking, Vault, Reports, Profile.
- **Lab (~6):** Registration, Dashboard, Test/Price Mgmt, Appointments, Reports.
- **Technician (~2):** Login, Task/Route Dashboard.
