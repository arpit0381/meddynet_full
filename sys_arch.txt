MeddyNet
National Diagnostics Discovery & Booking Platform
Tagline:Find, Compare, and Book Medical Tests Near You.
1. Platform Overview
MeddyNet is a location-based healthcare platform that connects users with nearby pathology labs, allowing them to:
• Search medical tests• Discover nearby labs on an interactive map• Compare prices• Book home sample collection• Book lab visit appointments• Store lifelong digital health records
The platform aggregates diagnostic labs and standardizes access to healthcare services.
2. Business Model
MeddyNet follows a subscription + commission model.
Revenue Sources
Lab Subscription Plans
Labs pay monthly/annual fees to appear on the platform.
Commission Per Test
MeddyNet charges 18% commission on every test booking.
Example:
Test price = ₹1000MeddyNet commission = ₹180Lab receives = ₹820
Premium Lab Visibility
Labs can appear higher in search results.
Health Packages
Preventive health subscription for users.
3. Lab Subscription Plans
Basic Plan
Best for small labs.
Features:
• Lab listing on platform• Test listing management• Appointment booking• Report upload• Basic analytics
Limitations:
• No priority listing• Limited technician support
Advanced Plan
Best for mid-size labs.
Includes:
• Everything in Basic• Priority search ranking• Home sample collection integration• Lab performance analytics• Technician management tools
Premium Plan
Best for large labs.
Includes:
• Everything in Advanced• Featured listing on homepage• marketing promotion inside app• AI demand analytics• API integration with lab systems
